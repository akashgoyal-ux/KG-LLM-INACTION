Apple Inc. (AAPL) Q3 2024 Earnings Call — CEO Tim Cook

Good afternoon. We are pleased to report another record quarter with revenue of $94.9 billion,
up 5% year-over-year. Services revenue reached an all-time high of $24.2 billion.
iPhone revenue was $46.0 billion for the quarter.

Our gross margin came in at 46.3%, driven by strong Services attach rates and continued
supply chain improvements. Net income was $21.4 billion, representing earnings per share
of $1.40, ahead of consensus estimates.

Looking ahead, we expect total revenue in the range of $94 to $98 billion for the December quarter,
reflecting continued momentum in India and growing traction of Apple Intelligence features
in iOS 18. We remain committed to returning value to shareholders: our Board has approved
an additional $110 billion share repurchase authorisation.

We do acknowledge headwinds from foreign exchange, particularly weakness in the Japanese yen
and the euro relative to the US dollar. We are also closely monitoring regulatory proceedings
in the European Union under the Digital Markets Act.
